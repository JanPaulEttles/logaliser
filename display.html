Simple. Extensible. Awesome.

logaliser allows you to search logs files for indicators of conpromise. logaliser is desgined to be easy to use, easy to extend and customise, and to limit the number of false positives.

Configurable

You can choose to run a range of different scans to look for specifics indicators. For instance you can search for only Credit Card numbers, or you can look for XSS attempts.

Free

Many companies pay vast amounts of money to analysis logs, logaliser is designed for those that need something that they can quickly and easily deploy at no cost.
